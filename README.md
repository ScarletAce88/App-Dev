**Transformers: Prime**

Transformers: Prime is an animated TV series that aired from 2010 to 2013. It tells the story of the never-ending war between the Autobots and the Decepticons as they battle for the fate of Earth and the universe. The series combines action, drama, and teamwork while exploring loyalty, power, and leadership.

**Main Characters**

Optimus Prime – The brave leader of the Autobots.
Bumblebee – A loyal scout with a strong sense of justice.
Megatron – The ruthless leader of the Decepticons.
Starscream – The sneaky and ambitious second-in-command.

**Favorite Character**

Megatron – He’s ruthless, intelligent, and determined to achieve victory no matter the cost. His presence brings tension and excitement to every episode, and his rivalry with Optimus Prime is one of the best parts of the series.

**Why I Love It**

The story has deep character development.
The fight scenes are intense and beautifully animated.          
The voice acting (especially Peter Cullen as Optimus) is incredible.
It balances action, emotion, and humor really well.

**Favorite Quote**

"Freedom is the right of all sentient beings."
— Optimus Prime
